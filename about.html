About

About Zeher Hassan

Zeher Hassan is an independent publication dedicated to in-depth analysis of the forces reshaping the modern world — from geopolitical rivalries and semiconductor supply chains to artificial intelligence and the technologies defining the next decade.

Our Mission

In a world of noise, clarity is rare. Our mission is to cut through the headlines and deliver well-researched, analytical content that helps readers understand not just what is happening — but why it matters and what comes next.

What We Cover

  • Geo-Politics — The shifting balance of power, regional conflicts, diplomatic developments, and the decisions of major state actors.
  • Semiconductors — The chip wars, supply chain vulnerabilities, and the nations and companies competing to control the foundational technology of the 21st century.
  • Deep-Tech — Artificial intelligence, quantum computing, biotechnology, and the emerging technologies shaping tomorrow.
  • Analysis — Long-form, in-depth reports that connect the dots across all of the above.

Editorial Standards

Every piece published on this site is researched and written with a commitment to accuracy and intellectual honesty. We do not publish rumour or speculation without clearly labelling it as such.

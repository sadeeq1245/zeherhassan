About The Desk

Operating at the intersection of Infrastructure, Deep-Tech, and Global Markets.

Zeher Hassan is a digital publication and intelligence desk dedicated to decoding the complex layers of modern technology, geopolitical shifts, and backend architecture.

Infrastructure & Architecture

The modern world runs on unseen infrastructure. Our focus lies heavily in analyzing the backbone of global tech—from advanced server deployments, Node.js and Redis clustering, to comprehensive cloud hosting environments on platforms like Hetzner, Oracle Cloud, and Google Cloud. We break down how data moves, how networks are engineered, and how autonomous agentic systems are reshaping the internet.

Markets & Geo-Politics

Technology does not exist in a vacuum. It is heavily influenced by, and exerts influence over, global financial markets and state policies. We track the flow of capital and data, analyzing trends across digital asset platforms, traditional equity indexes, and the physical hardware supply chains that dictate market dominance.

The Mission

To provide high-signal, low-noise intelligence. Whether it is a deep dive into the firmware of network antennas or a macroeconomic breakdown of tech supply chains, our dispatches are engineered for professionals who need to understand the structural realities of the digital and physical world.
